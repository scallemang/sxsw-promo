Synbiota's mission is to standardize biotechnology and make it accessible to everyone.

Synbiota is a set of rapid prototyping tools that move synthetic biology out of institutions, unversities, and large corporations, by lowering the threshold of entry for doing it and using it to solve problems.

Synbiota is a software and wetware platform that offers the ability to start using the latest in synthetic biology tools to do your work faster and make it sharable.

Through use of standardized DNA parts that can be rapidly assembled like LEGO blocks, you can use and assemble a growing list of standardized DNA parts and build complex genetic circuits in hours rather than weeks or months.

The standardized DNA parts are complemented with a DNA design software for designing and conceptualizing your genetic circuits before you assemble them. Synbiota's electronic lab book is host protocols and gives you the ability to log your experimental results and relate it back to the DNA you designed, assembled, and tested.
