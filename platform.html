Synbiota Platform

Synbiota is an easy and quick method to learn and build with DNA.

It’s an integrated software and wetware platform, designed to enable an ever-growing library of standardized DNA parts which communicate to one another using the Rapid DNA Prototyping Standard to build robust genetic constructs—so researchers are free to create, rather than spend valuable time and resources developing parts and constructs from scratch.

Synbiota brings process improvement to life science, enabling an engineer’s approach to biology, and the distribuition and sharing of parts. All of this is brought together in an integrated feedback loop connecting the creation of parts with advanced software, the distribuition and sharing of those parts in a virtual marketplace, and real-world testing and assembly using compatible wetware.
